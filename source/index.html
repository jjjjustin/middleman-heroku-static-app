Toolio

What it does:

Ladders, lawn mowers, wheel barrows, saws - there's a wealth of tools just begging to be shared in our neighborhoods and communities

Toolio connects friends and neighbors to allow easy lending and borrowing of common or uncommon household tools - sign up, list the tools you'd like to lend, and search for anything you might need to borrow!


How it works:

Toolio is built to be accesible - once you've created your account and found the tool you need, simply click on it and make a reservation. Toolio's built in messaging service connects users so that any and all contact is made at each user's discretion - you can choose who you connect with, and are free to cancel contact at any time. Once the transcation is complete, we urge all users to rate each other so we can build a solid and verified network of lenders and borrowers!

Project Details:

  • Details: Built with Ruby on Rails/PostgreSQL
  • Status: Work in progress
  • Intended launch: Summer 2015
  • Live site: Toolio

Efficientize

What it does:

The end goal is to provide both home and business owners a clear assessment of their potential to save money by upgrading lighting, HVAC, insulation, and other basic infrastructure components.
Some upgrades pay themselves off in weeks or months, some take years to begin having a marked effect on ernergy savings - Efficientize will show you what upgrades give the best return, and will help conect users with any and all government incentives to perfrom these upgrades.

How it works:

Users will be prompted to perform a basic efficiency audit on their home or workspace and will then receive estimates on potential savings over a specified period of time - Efficientize will also connect users with any and all available rebates, loans, and other incentives provided by state/local/federal government programs as well as offers from local utility companies. Efficientize functions both as an aggregator of available incentives and as a means to calculate savings and determine the value of upgrades over time.

Project Details:

  • Details: Built with Ruby on Rails/PostgresQL
  • Status: Work in progress
  • Intended launch: Summer 2015
  • Live site: Efficientize

Reservatron 5000

What it does:

Reservatron provides a means for students and teachers to reserve class and meeting spaces at our schools campus - it was designed as an in-class project to make the arrangement of extracurricular activities easier to manage

How it works:

Users simply create accounts, view the available spaces - if the room is available on the date/s required users are free to make a reservation - this is logged into the database and the room will no longer be available at the given date.

Project Details:

  • Details: Built with Ruby on Rails/PostgresQL
  • Status: Completed
  • Launch: Winter 2015
  • Live site: Reservatron 5000
